What do we do?

We aim to reduce the impact of air pollution on Chiang Mai

We are a non-profit organization that aims to improve the air quality for the people of Chiang Mai, to mitigate the impact of air pollution on the environment and on human life. We believe that everyone deserves to have clean air to breath and we want to make this a reality for the people of Chiang Mai. We do our best to provide clean air to all people in Chiang Mai and support the decline in Chiang Mai air pollution.

One of the main ways we do this is by fundraising money for the development and distribution of systems such as air purifers, positive pressure systems, and air quality sensors, as well as research that goes towards understanding the causes and effects of the smoke in Chiang Mai, hopefully leading towards lowered future air pollution levels and understanding how to best mitigate the risks and impacts of air pollution on Chiang Mai.

We act as an intermediary between the people of Chiang Mai (you) and those who are working their hardest on providing clean air for everyone in Chiang Mai. We work towards developing, designing, and distributing low cost positive pressure systems, air filters and purifiers, and sensors for groups that need to be protected from the toxic air such as schools, and groups who canâ€™t afford expensive systems such as positive pressure systems. We also work with leading researchers and policy makers at institutions such as at Chiang Mai Rajhabat University (CMRU) and Chiang Mai University (CMU) to work on ways to finally improve the air in Chiang Mai.
