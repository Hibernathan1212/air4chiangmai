##Air4ChiangMai non-profit website

The website for a non-profit I created called "Air4ChiangMai" where we aim to lessen the impact of air pollution on Chiang Mai

***The website will be at air4chiangmai.me***

Gives information on our non-profit, such as our main goals, who we are, why we do it, and how we are going to achieve our goal.
