What do we do?

We aim to reduce the impact of air pollution on Chiang Mai

We aim to improve the air for people of Chiang Mai, doing our best to provide clean air for all.We work with leading researchers and policy makers at institutions such as at Chiang Mai Rajhabat Univeristy and Chiang Mai Univeristy to work on ways to finally improve the air in Chiang Mai.

We also work with organizations that design, develop, and distribute positive pressure systems, sensors, air filters, and more for schools and groups that need to be protected from the toxic air.

Who are we?

A non-profit ran by high school students in Chiang Mai

We are a non-profit organization ran by high school students who want to make a difference in the community. We are passionate about the environment and understand and have seen the impact and destruction that air pollution has caused in Chiang Mai.

We strive towards improving air for the population of Chiang Mai and are dedicated to doing our best in solving the problem. We are committed to making a difference and we are working hard to make a positive impact on the community and environment.

Why do we do it?

We believe that everyone has the right to clean air

We believe that everyone has the right to clean air and that access to clean air is a basic human right. We want to allow for the people of Chiang Mai, especially those who are most vulnerable such as those at a young age, to have access to clean air. Air pollution is a significant issue that needs to be dealt with, having serious consequences on the environment, wellbeing, and lives of many.
